About Ioulia's Tour

Learn more about our story and our passion for showing you the best of Crete.

At Ioulia’s Tour, we don’t just show you Crete—we share the island we know and love as if you were family.

We are a Greek and an Italian who have spent every summer in Crete for the past 30 years. Over the decades, we have explored every corner of this incredible island—its breathtaking beaches, charming villages, ancient sites, and hidden culinary gems. Crete is more than just a destination for us; it’s a place we’ve come to know deeply, and now, we want to share that passion with others.

With our comfortable minivan and a love for storytelling, we personally guide each tour, creating authentic, tailor-made experiences for travelers who want more than just a standard excursion. Whether you’re looking to relax on secluded beaches, dive into Crete’s rich history, or indulge in the island’s world-renowned cuisine, we craft each journey to match your interests.

We don’t follow rigid itineraries or rush from one site to the next. Instead, we take the time to explore at your pace, uncovering hidden gems and sharing the stories and traditions that make Crete so special.

So, if you're looking for a warm, personalized, and unforgettable adventure, hop in our minivan and let us take you on a journey through the Crete we love.

Let’s explore Crete together—one unforgettable trip at a time!
