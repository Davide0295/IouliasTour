Escursioni e Servizi Shuttle da Akrotiri/Chania

Offriamo servizi privati di noleggio con conducente (NCC) e shuttle condivisi, con partenza esclusiva dalla zona di Akrotiri/Chania. Le nostre escursioni sono confortevoli, sicure e ideali per gruppi e famiglie. Prenotazione anticipata consigliata.

Day Trips

Falasarna - Beach Day (min 4 people)

Highlights

  • Discover one of the most iconic beaches on the west coast
  • Enjoy spectacular sunsets
  • Swim in turquoise, crystal-clear waters
  • Stop at charming villages along the way

Full Description

Enjoy a trip to one of Crete’s most enchanting spots: Falasarna. Renowned for its golden sandy beach and turquoise sea, the transfer is provided in an air-conditioned minivan as a private service (min 4 people), ensuring comfort throughout the journey. Relax, swim and sunbathe at your leisure.

Times & Itinerary

8:00-8:30 - Pick-up

We’ll collect you from the Akrotiri/Chania area (multiple stops may be required).

10:00 - Arrival at Falasarna

Arrival at the beach with plenty of free time to relax and swim.

17:30 - Depart for Chania

Departure from Falasarna with drop-off at designated points in Chania by 19:00.

Includes

  • Transport in an air-conditioned minivan (Private Service)
  • Experienced driver
  • Pickup and drop-off from designated areas in Akrotiri/Chania
  • Suggestions on local spots and restaurants

Elafonisi - The Pink Sand Beach (min 4 people)

Highlights

  • Spend a relaxing day on the pink sand beaches of Elafonisi
  • Swim in crystal-clear waters
  • Stroll in a protected area of outstanding natural beauty
  • Take in lush green landscapes through Topolia Gorge

Full Description

Following pickup by minivan, journey to the southwest corner of Crete to enjoy a private, relaxing day on the pink sand beaches of the small island of Elafonisi. Experience breathtaking natural surroundings with plenty of free time to swim, sunbathe and unwind.

Times & Itinerary

8:00-8:30 - Pick-up

Arrival at the beach with ample time to relax and swim.

Shuttle Services

Shuttle to Marathi Beach

A shared service in an air-conditioned minivan. Departure at 9:00 AM and return by 7:00 PM. Ideal for families (minimum 4 people).

*Pickup and drop-off times can be personalized and flexible upon request.*

Shuttle to Agia Triada Monastery

A dedicated shuttle service to Agia Triada Monastery, located a few kilometers outside of Chania. Departure at 12:00 and return at 16:00. Ideal for families (minimum 4 people). Highlights: Visit the monastery renowned for producing organic olive oil, wine, honey and soap. Optional: Enjoy a tasting of exquisite organic wines.

Evening Shuttle to Chania Center

Perfect for a night out in town: dinner, a stroll along the Venetian port, or shopping. Depart at 8:30 PM, return by 12:30 AM. Shared service with a minimum of 4 people.

*Pickup and drop-off times can be personalized and flexible upon request.*
